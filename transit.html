Planet Details

The Transit Method is a clever way of deducing information like Exoplanet Size and Orbital Period based on changes in a Host Star's light emission. This is by keeping track of the level of light emitted from a Host Star, as dips in this level would be caused by a exoplanet passing in front of it, blocking part of it. Based on data like duration of decrease in light, and intensity of that decrease, we can deduce the size and orbital period of these exoplanets.
